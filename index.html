featured places

Jaipur, Rajasthan

Jaipur, the capital city of Rajasthan, is known for its rich history and stunning architecture, including the iconic Hawa Mahal and Amer Fort.

Jodhpur, Rajasthan

Jodhpur, often referred to as the "Blue City" of Rajasthan, is famous for its azure-hued buildings that contrast beautifully with the golden desert landscape.

Udaipur, Rajasthan

Udaipur, known as the "City of Lakes" in Rajasthan, is celebrated for its enchanting setting around pristine lakes

Chittorgarh, Rajasthan

Chittorgarh, with its formidable Chittorgarh Fort, is a city steeped in history, known for its resilience and tales of valor

Jaisalmer, Rajasthan

Jaisalmer, the "Golden City" of Rajasthan, is a desert oasis known for its stunning sandstone architecture that shimmers in the desert sun.

Mount Abu, Rajasthan

Mount Abu, Rajasthan's only hill station, is a serene retreat known for its cool climate, lush greenery, and the spectacular Dilwara Jain Temples.
